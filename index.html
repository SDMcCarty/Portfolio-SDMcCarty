About Me

When my childhood dream of being a tiger was dashed, I contemplated my decidedly human life and discovered I love helping. Upon graduating from university, I moved to Sendai, Japan to teach in a public school. After the earthquake/tsunami/near-nuclear-disaster, we teachers ran the evacuation site. That experience rekindled my desire to help. Computers help in countless ways. I wanted in on that helping action, and began my coding journey.

When not coding, I enjoy art, writing, video games, and cooking. I am a published artist and author, and the undisputed Spice Queen amongst my friends.

Animal Group Quiz App

This REACT-ful JavaScript app was created to test animal lover's knowledge of what certain animal groups are called. Every since learning what a group of bunnies is called, I've been very interested in learning about other animal groups. Some are very apropos (the rhino, which is in the test, so no spoilers!), and some are slightly outlandish (a group of giraffes is called a tower!), most animal group names are interesting to learn about. From start to finish, users select their answer, are given the correct one, and finally shown their final score before being asked if they'd like to try it again. Give it a try - hopefully we'll kindle (a group of kittens) your interest!

About Me

When my childhood dream of being a tiger was dashed, I contemplated my decidedly human life and discovered I love helping. Upon graduating from university, I moved to Sendai, Japan to teach in a public school. After the earthquake/tsunami/near-nuclear-disaster, we teachers ran the evacuation site. That experience rekindled my desire to help. Computers help in countless ways. I wanted in on that helping action, and began my coding journey.

When not coding, I enjoy art, writing, video games, and cooking. I am a published artist and author, and the undisputed Spice Queen amongst my friends.

Clerc - Spaced Repetition ASL Learning App

Have you ever wanted to learn a language, but been stuck on how to go about it? With Clerc, you can use the tried and true method of spaced repetition to learn ASL (American Sign Language)! Clerc keeps track of your correct responses, and which words are causing a bit more trouble. The total score feature helps you keep track of how you are doing, and makes it easy to see your improvement!

My Clientele

Many companies have strict guidelines on how to display their products, and keeping up with the current needs of clients can be troublesome while on the go. With My Clientele, you can keep all your client information in one place. This includes names, locations, hours of operations, if they are currently closed, and the current general manager. You can even update the clients as this information changes. With the click of a button, you are able to write reports with pictures right from your mobile device. Go back and look at past reports to help easily spot trends and common places a client needs help wtih. My Clientele is perfect for the fast-paced lifestyle we lead!

Animal Group Quiz App

This REACT-ful JavaScript app was created to test animal lover's knowledge of what certain animal groups are called. Every since learning what a group of bunnies is called, I've been very interested in learning about other animal groups. Some are very apropos (the rhino, which is in the test, so no spoilers!), and some are slightly outlandish (a group of giraffes is called a tower!), most animal group names are interesting to learn about. From start to finish, you can select your answer, get the correct one, and finally you can see your final score before being asked if you'd like to try again. Give it a try - hopefully we'll kindle (a group of kittens) your interest!
